# AI Skills Essential According to SAP Survey

A recent SAP survey highlights the critical importance of AI skills in the modern workforce, revealing that organizations are prioritizing AI competency development.

## Summary

A recent SAP survey highlights the critical importance of AI skills in the modern workforce, revealing that organizations are prioritizing AI competency development to remain competitive in the digital economy.

The survey findings indicate a significant shift in skill requirements across industries, with AI literacy becoming a fundamental competency for professionals at all levels, from entry-level positions to senior leadership roles.

### Key Survey Findings

*   **High Demand:** 85% of organizations report increased demand for AI-related skills across all departments.
*   **Skill Gap:** 70% of companies identify a significant gap between current employee skills and required AI competencies.
*   **Investment Priority:** 90% of organizations plan to increase investment in AI skills training and development programs.
*   **Competitive Advantage:** Companies with strong AI skill sets report 40% higher productivity and innovation metrics.

### Essential AI Skills

The survey identifies several core AI skills that are becoming essential across various roles: AI literacy and understanding, data analysis and interpretation, prompt engineering, AI tool utilization, and ethical AI practices.

Organizations are increasingly looking for professionals who can effectively collaborate with AI systems, understand AI capabilities and limitations, and apply AI solutions to solve business problems creatively and ethically.

**Source:** SAP Global AI Skills Survey 2025
